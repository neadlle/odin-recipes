Seafood Sausage

This is an oceanic take on the classic land animal sausage!

Pictured above is an image of a classic land animal sausage, but just pretend that it's a seafood sausage.

Or just more familiarly called "Seasage"

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Salmon
  • Breadcrumbs (I think)
  • Salt and Pepper, probably
  • Other

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a food processor
  2. Wrap mush in saran wrap
  3. Wrap saran wrapped mush in tin foil
  4. Boil tin foiled mush
  5. Unwrap
  6. Pan Fry
  7. Enjoy!
